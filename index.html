My path to IT
With my roots in art and music, I've always been thrilled when indulging in creative tasks. When growing up I also felt the need for structure, and logic, therefor I was drawn to the field of engineering, where science and art is merged. During my Vehicle Engineering studies at The Royal Institute of Technology, KTH, I was also interested in the environment and decided to chose a masters in Sustainable Energy Technology. This lead me to my previous dream job, developing gas turbines for power plants. After some years in this field, I changed my focus to AI and machine learning, which lead me to a job at the scandinavian bank SEB where I today work with their Virtual Assistants and IT architecture in general. In my free time I've been learning front end development, with a future goal of becoming a fullstack developer.
